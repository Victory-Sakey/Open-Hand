ABOUT US

Our Mission

OPEN HAND HEALTHCARE SERVICES INC is a 24-hour home care service specifically established and designed to provide quality cost-effective in-home nursing and personal care for the geriatric and medically impaired community. Our services make it possible to maintain as much independence in the home as possible.

By providing the appropriate in-home assistance and companionship, we aim to lift the spirits of not only our clients, but also of the family and caregivers. It is OPEN HAND HEALTHCARE SERVICES INC mission to provide the highest quality care and services that meet and exceed the expectations of the patient. OPEN HAND HEALTHCARE SERVICES INC is dedicated to hold paramount the welfare of the patient as we promote and enhance their quality of life.

Our Services

what we do here

Open Hand Healthcare services , you have the advantage and convenience of receiving the best level of care at competitive pricings. Some of our supreme services consist of but are not limited to the following: chore services, companionship services, personal care services.

Chore Services
Housekeeping services which substitute for the regular activities necessary to maintain a safe, clean, healthy environment. Chore services include but are not limited to the following: Light housekeeping, Laundry, Linen Changes, Vacuuming, Pet Care, Meal Preparation, Correspondence, Taking out trash, Holiday decorations
Companionship Services
From daily telephone calls from a “Buddy”, to daily “Friendly” visitor, to round the clock paid companions. Some of our companionship services are as follows: Conversation, Walks, Friendship, Laughter, reading a good book, mental stimulation, exploring life stories, enjoying new experiences, Lifting spirits
Personal Care Services
Services which substitutes for the bodily activities of daily living which an individual would normally provide for himself/herself. Personal Care Services may include, but are not necessarily limited to, the following services provided for the client: Bathing, Dressing, Feeding, Transferring, Care of teeth, skin, hair, and/or nails, Light meal preparation, Toileting, Ambulation
Respite Services
A trained volunteer or para-professional stays with your loved one and take over your care giving role, whether for several hours or several days. These brief reprieves from a care giving situation are healthy for the caregiver and also for the care recipient as a change of daily routine for both.
Complex Care Services
The agency shall provide complex care services, wound care, tube feedings, administration of IV, Trach care and ventilator services.
Transportation Services
Transporting to and from the following: Medical appointments, Shopping, Adult Day Centers
